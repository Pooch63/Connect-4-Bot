Connect 4 Bot

About

I made this bot in early 8th grade. I lost our homeroom's Connect 4 tournament, and my science teacher started making fun of me.
He had to pay.


How it works:

This is a simple Connect 4 bot that uses minimax. It uses an array to store the board position. After this bot, I spent a few weeks making a totally different one written in TypeScript. I wanted to use 64 bit integers to store the board info, like in Pascal Pons' C++ version. Since you can only use bitwise operations on 32-bit integers in JS (so weird, right?), I had to use Bigints.

Yeah, suffice to say, I didn't get very far with that. So, thus far, arrays seem to be the way to go, and this bot triumphs over every other attempt of mine at a JS/TS bot.
